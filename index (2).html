About Carson Car Concierge

Meet Monty

Carson Car Concierge was founded by Monty Carson, driven by a genuine passion for cars and a commitment to helping people navigate the car-buying process with confidence. Since launching the business in 2020, Monty has turned that passion into a trusted service for clients seeking transparency and value.

In his early 20s, Monty had a negative experience at a dealership — overpaying due to hidden fees, negative equity, and an excessively high interest rate. That experience became the driving force behind Carson Car Concierge.

Today, the mission is simple: ensure no client goes through what he did. Carson Car Concierge advocates for its clients every step of the way — negotiating the best possible deals while saving them time, money, stress, and uncertainty. With industry knowledge and a client-first approach, Monty works to level the playing field and deliver a smarter, smoother car-buying experience.
